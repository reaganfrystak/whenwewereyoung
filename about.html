About Us

When We Were Young festival in Las Vegas is a legendary pop punk and emo festival that began in 2022. With five different stages and over 50 bands rocking out on a 12-hour cycle each day, WWWY taps into the angsty hearts of emo/alternative music lovers by showcasing the top lovers of that scene in one eventful weekend.

Dozens of your favorite artists,

one unforgettable weekend

Since WWWY’s inception, it has been our goal to connect you with the loved artists of your youth. Some artists who have performed at this festival include (but aren’t limited to):

- My Chemical Romance - Green Day - Avril Lavigne - Fall Out Boy - Panic! At The Disco - The Offspring - Paramore - Weezer - Pierce The Veil
